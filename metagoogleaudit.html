Investment budget range - USD 150-200K / 200-300K / 300-400K / 400K plus. Hard qualifier - filters below studio entry price USD 169K.

Overseas property experience - Calibrates call depth. First-timers need education; experienced investors need proof points.
5
Country of residence - Routes EE, LV, LT leads to Estonian-language follow-up automatically.

Adding 5 questions will increase CPL by 20-40%. This is intentional. A qualified lead at EUR 80 CPL is worth 10x an unqualified lead at EUR 20 CPL at this AOV. The sales team's time is the bottleneck, not lead volume.
